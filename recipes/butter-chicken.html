Butter Chicken

Description

This is a family favorite butter chicken recipe. It was adapted from Nagi Maehashi's webite, who adapted it from Luke Mangan. If you haven't checked out Nagi's website, Reciptin Eats, you definitely should.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup full fat plain yogurt
  • 2 TBSP lemon juice
  • 2 tsp tumeric powder
  • 4 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 TBSP freshly grated ginger OR 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 3 pounds chicken thighs cut into bite sized pieces
  • 2 cups tomato puree (tomato passata)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 TBSP sugar
  • 2.5tsp salt (fine sea salt)

Steps

  1. Combine yogurt, lemon guice, tumeric powder, garam masala, cayenne pepper, ground cumin, ginger, and chicken and marinade for 3-24 hours. You can skip this step and just start cooking if you don't want to wait.
  2. Cook the mixutre in about 4TBSP of olive oil over high heat until the chicken turns white all over.
  3. Add the tomato puree/ passata, sugar, and salt. Turn to low and simmer 20 minutes.
  4. Add heavy cream and bring back up to temp.
  5. Gardnish with cilantro if you want.

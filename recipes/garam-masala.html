Garam Masala

Description

This is the spice mixuture used in the Butter Chicken recipe. This will make several batches of butter chicken. You can find this already mixed up, but it is cheaper and more consistent to just do it yourself.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup ground cumin
  • 1/4 cup ground coriander
  • 2 tablespons ground cardamom
  • 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 4 teaspoons ground cinnamom
  • 2 teaspoons ground cloves
  • 2 teaspoons ground nutmeg

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a mason jar or other airtight contanier.
